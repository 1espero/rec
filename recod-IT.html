Diploma in Health Records and Information Technology

City College proudly offers a Diploma in Health Records and Information Technology, a program that prepares students to manage and maintain critical healthcare information systems. This course bridges the gap between healthcare and technology, equipping graduates with the skills needed to streamline medical record management and support efficient healthcare delivery. The program spans three years, starting at Level 4, advancing to Level 5, and culminating at Level 6, with each level comprising two semesters.

Program Structure

  • Academic Calendar: Classes commence in October and conclude in August, with end-of-semester examinations to assess academic understanding and practical skills.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Key areas of study include health data management, medical terminology, database systems, electronic health records, coding and classification, and healthcare informatics.

Why Choose Our Diploma in Health Records and Information Technology Program?

1. Expert Faculty

Our instructors are experienced professionals in health records management and IT, offering students a comprehensive understanding of both fields.

2. Hands-On Training

Students gain practical experience with industry-standard software and tools used in health information systems, ensuring they are job-ready upon graduation.

3. Focus on Data Accuracy and Security

The program emphasizes the importance of maintaining accurate records and ensuring data privacy, critical for effective healthcare operations.

4. Expansive Career Opportunities

Graduates can work in hospitals, clinics, research institutions, insurance companies, and public health organizations as health information technicians, medical coders, or data analysts. The program also provides a foundation for advanced studies in health informatics.

Additional Benefits

  • State-of-the-Art Facilities: City College offers modern computer labs and access to health information software to support practical learning.
  • Supportive Learning Environment: Students benefit from personalized mentorship, collaborative projects, and opportunities to engage in real-world scenarios.
  • Affordable Education: Our program combines quality education with affordability, ensuring access to professional training for all aspiring health information specialists.
